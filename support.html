Troubleshooting Tips

  • App won't open a video? Ensure your video file is in a supported format (typically .mp4 or .mov) and that you have granted Photos/Files permissions in your device's Settings > Privacy.
  • Export failing? Ensure your device has enough free storage space to create the temporary zip file, especially if you are processing large batches of video.
  • Need to reset the app? If you encounter unexpected behavior, try force-closing the app and restarting your device.
  • Permissions: If you accidentally denied permission to your Photo Library, go to your iPhone's Settings > ML Data Factory and toggle Photos to "Full Access."
